# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully and collaborate constructively.
Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or abusive behavior are not tolerated.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical discussions.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions when needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Threats, intimidation, or sustained hostility
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments/issues/PRs that violate this policy.
Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Scope

This policy applies to project repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and associated chat spaces.
